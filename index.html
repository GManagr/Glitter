About Glitter


At GCC we aim to simplify the lifestyle of common people using computation and hence add a value of computing to their life.


Computing enhances human capabilities.we build products having the immense power of computation. A product that aims to simplify the lifestyle of people through the application of computation. Our products can be use by anyone at anytime. We are making here the tools for mind so that people use it in their daily life and hence more using computation to solve their problem.


Our Values


MISSION: Glitter mission is to create, facilitate, and manage a cognitive community, where all people work cohesively with same enthusiasm to solve the problem.


VISION: Technology continues to changes our perception of it's. We can make technology integrate more into an individual's lifestyle. Computer and Software are the tools of the mind. Our Vision is to simplify the lifestyle of common people through computation and building products that are more integrated into the lifestyle.
